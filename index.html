What We Build

Toolbox is a suite of desktop toolbars built for MSPs. One lives on your help desk, the other with your clients. Both are designed to make support faster, clearer, and less interruptive.

Frontline

A simple, client-facing toolbar in the taskbar for easy support requests and updates.

  • Log new tickets with one click
  • Check ticket status and request updates
  • Rate recent service directly in the toolbar
  • Launch onboarding/offboarding tools
  • Fully branded per client
